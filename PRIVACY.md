# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

## Overview

Nev is a beta productivity application for planning projects, notes, and weekly schedules.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected and how it may be handled when you use the app.

## Information We May Collect

Depending on how the app is configured, we may collect and store:

- account details such as your email address
- authentication data managed through Supabase Auth
- planner content you create, including projects, notes, deadlines, and scheduling information
- basic technical/session information needed to operate the service

## How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

- authenticate you
- save and sync planner content
- provide access across sessions and devices
- improve, maintain, and debug the beta service

## Storage

Planner content may be stored:

- locally in your browser
- in Supabase when cloud sync is enabled

If cloud sync is enabled, your planner data is associated with your authenticated account.

## Third-Party Services

The app may rely on third-party providers, including:

- Supabase for authentication and database storage
- Vercel for hosting and deployment
- OAuth providers such as Google if enabled

Those services may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

## Retention

Data may be retained as long as needed to operate the beta service, unless removed by the operator or deleted at your request.

## Deletion

If you would like your account or planner data removed, contact the app operator.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored information, but no storage or transmission method is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13.

## Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.
